## Customization

### Text Space Position
- **Left:** `subject at far right, vast empty area on left`
- **Right:** `subject at far left edge, expansive space on right`
- **Top:** `subject in lower portion, large empty sky/space above`
- **Center:** `subject at edges of frame, large invisible empty center area`

### Mood Adjustment
- **More dramatic:** Add "dramatic", "cinematic", "epic" + increase `--s` to 200-300
- **More calm:** Add "serene", "peaceful", "gentle" + "soft diffused lighting"
- **More mysterious:** Add "mysterious", "enigmatic" + "fog", "mist" + `--weird 50-100`

### Brand Colors
```
[your prompt], color palette of deep navy blue and gold accents
```

### Animation-Ready
Add `--tile` for seamless looping. Best subjects: abstract forms, cosmic scenes, smoke/fog, water, clouds.
